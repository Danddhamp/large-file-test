Common Questions in Regards to Prophets

Home Page

1 What is a Prophet?

A prophet is anyone that acts and/or speaks for God. Prophets often get conflated with visionaries, though visionaries can indeed be Prophets. On this website, we particularly will look at the Prophets in the Canonized Scriptures and those who are sustained as prophets, seers and revelators (Aposltes and Presidents of the Church).

2 Do Prophets Matter?

Yes. Amos 3:7. God reveals His secrets through the Prophets. God guides us as a body of Saints through His Prophets.

3 Does God still call Prophets?

Yes.
